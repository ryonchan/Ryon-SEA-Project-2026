My love for Singapore Cuisine

Hi Snap Academy Team!

My name is Ryon, and I grew up in a tiny city-state called Singapore (a vast 8600 miles away!). This is a food catalog page which I have specially curated displaying my recommendations for the best street food stalls in Singapore, also known as 'hawker' stalls. Hawker stalls encapsulate a significant part of my identity, as it represents food arising from Singapore's melting pot of cultures : Chinese, Malay, Indian, Peranakan. Every stall typically sell a single, distinct and perfected item at an affordable price. Recipes have been passed down for generations and kept a highly affordable price to continue serving the community. Every weekend, my family and I used to indulge in a feast of different hawker food items (they are usually only $3-6USD)

After moving to California for college, I realized how much I missed those memories. I hope this catalog shares to the Snap Team a little about where I came from — 50 stalls that define what Singapore tastes like.

From $2 curry puffs to Michelin-starred pork noodles, every stall here has earned its place through decades of waking at dawn, perfecting one dish, and feeding generations of Singaporeans.

— Made by Ryon (while hungry)
